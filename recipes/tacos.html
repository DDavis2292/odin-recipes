Shredded Beef Tacos

Description

This recipe is for slowly braised beef tacos, topped with queso fresco, pickled red onion,
avocado, and crema will leave your mouth watering. All you'll need are the following
ingredients and you'll have delicious tacos for the entire family:

Ingredients

  • Beef Chuck Roast
  • Corn Tortillas (yellow or white)
  • Queso Fresco Cheese
  • Avocado
  • Cilantro
  • Mexican Crema
  • Pickled Red Onion
  • Limes

Steps

  1. Season the chuck roast with your choice of spices and sear on all sides
  2. Place the chuck roast in a covered dutch oven, and roast at 325 degrees F for 3 to 4 hours
  3. When you beef is fork tender, shred all of beef into bit sized pieces
  4. Take your tortillas and place them on a flat top, in a hot pan, or over an open flame to get
    them warm and even a little chared
  5. Place your shredded beef in your warm tortilla
  6. Top your taco with your fresh Mexican crema, queso fresco, avocado, and your pickled onion.
  7. Last but not least, don't for get to squeeze a little fresh lime over top and garnish with cilantro!

ENJOY!
